Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Duplicate Photo - File Remover is built with a Privacy-First approach. We believe your personal files should stay exactly where they belong: on your device.

This app is a fully offline utility. We do not use any cloud servers to process, scan, or store your data.

Core Principles

  • Local Processing: All duplicate detection and file analysis happens entirely on your phone's processor.
  • Zero Data Collection: We do not collect names, emails, or usage statistics.
  • User Sovereignty: You have full control over which files are scanned and which are deleted.

Permissions & Usage

To provide its core functionality, the app requires specific permissions. Here is exactly why we need them:

Media & Storage Access Essential for scanning your storage to find duplicate photos, videos, and documents. Without this, the app cannot see the files it needs to clean.
All Files Access (Android 11+) Required to find duplicates in system folders, downloads, and specialized file types (like APKs) that are hidden from standard apps. This is optional but recommended for a thorough clean.
Contacts Access Only used if you explicitly choose to scan for duplicate contacts. We do not sync or export your contact list.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing for us to sell or share with advertisers or third-party companies. Your privacy is guaranteed by the offline nature of the app.

Security Measures

Your data is protected by the standard Android security sandbox. Because the app does not have background internet upload capabilities for your files, your data cannot be leaked to the web by this application.
